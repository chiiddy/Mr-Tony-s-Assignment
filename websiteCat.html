Cat Fancy Event 2023

The 115th annual Cat Fancy Event is being held in New York
City on December 21st. This is the faciest cat event of the year,
starring over 300 different cats.This event was made to show
Americans how great cats really are. There will be treat at the
walk in, and for the cats owner there will be buffet in the
lounge. We thank you for coming to our event site, and hope to
see you on December 21st. Thank you
